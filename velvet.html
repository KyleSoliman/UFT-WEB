What is the Velvet Room?

The Velvet Room is a mysterious interdimensional space that exists between dream and reality, mind and matter. It serves as a sanctuary for those with the Wild Card ability, allowing them to access powers beyond normal comprehension. The room's appearance changes based on the guest's circumstances and mental state, reflecting their journey's current phase.
👨‍🦲

Igor - The Master

Igor is the enigmatic master of the Velvet Room, a long-nosed man who has served countless guests throughout time. He possesses vast knowledge of Personas and the collective unconscious. Despite his unsettling appearance, Igor is a benevolent guide who helps Wild Card users understand their power and navigate the challenges ahead.
⚗️

Persona Fusion

The primary service of the Velvet Room is Persona Fusion - the art of combining multiple Personas to create new, more powerful ones. This alchemical process allows Wild Card users to expand their arsenal and adapt to any situation. Advanced fusion techniques can create legendary Personas with devastating abilities.
👥

The Attendants

The Velvet Room attendants are artificial beings created by Igor to assist guests. In Persona 5 Royal, the twin wardens Caroline and Justine serve this role, though they initially view the protagonist as a prisoner. These attendants help manage the Persona compendium and provide various services to aid the guest's rehabilitation.
📚

Persona Compendium

The Persona Compendium is a mystical registry that records every Persona the Wild Card user has encountered or created. It serves as both a historical record and practical tool, allowing users to resummon previously obtained Personas for a fee. This invaluable resource ensures that no hard-earned power is ever truly lost.

🎭 The Velvet Room's True Purpose

Beyond its practical services, the Velvet Room represents a space of personal growth and rehabilitation. It challenges guests to confront their inner selves, overcome psychological barriers, and ultimately achieve their full potential. The room itself evolves with the guest, transforming from prison to limousine to reflect their journey toward freedom and self-actualization.
